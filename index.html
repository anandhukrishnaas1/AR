Gradually face your fears in safe, immersive VR environments. Choose a phobia below to begin your journey toward recovery.

Therapy Sessions

Each session has 3 intensity levels. Start easy and progress at your own pace.

🗣️

Social Anxiety

Endure a police interrogation with blinding lights, anchor a live TV broadcast to 14 million viewers — then address a World Summit of 500+ leaders from 194 nations.

Level 1: Interrogation Room Level 2: Live TV Broadcast Level 3: World Arena Summit
🏔️

Acrophobia

Stand on a collapsing 300m skyscraper as debris falls, balance on an active volcano rim above molten lava — then float on a disintegrating space station 400km above Earth.

Level 1: Collapsing Skyscraper (300m) Level 2: Volcano Rim (3,700m) Level 3: Space Station (400km)
🌊

Aquaphobia

Watch a 30m tsunami wall approach the shore, dive 120m to a shark-circled shipwreck in darkness — then descend to the Mariana Trench in a cracking submarine at 10,916m.

Level 1: Tsunami Approach Level 2: Shipwreck Abyss (120m) Level 3: Mariana Trench (10,916m)
🌑

Nyctophobia

Navigate a blackout hospital with flickering lights and shadow figures, descend into skull-lined catacombs with a dying torch — then face The Void: absolute nothingness where something watches.

Level 1: Abandoned Hospital Level 2: Underground Catacombs Level 3: The Void

How It Works

1

Choose a Phobia

Select the fear you want to work on from the sessions above.

2

Pick a Level

Start with the easiest level and build up gradually.

3

Choose Your Device

Meta Quest: Click VR icon for full immersion. Laptop: Use mouse + WASD to explore.

4

Breathe & Progress

Use the built-in breathing guide. Move to the next level when ready.
