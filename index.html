Gradually face your fears in safe, immersive VR environments. Choose a phobia below to begin your journey toward recovery.

Therapy Sessions

Each session has 3 intensity levels. Start easy and progress at your own pace.

🗣️

Social Anxiety

Practice being in social situations — from a quiet café to a crowded auditorium stage.

Level 1: Café Level 2: Classroom Level 3: Stage
🏔️

Acrophobia

Overcome fear of heights — from a low balcony to a mountain cliff edge.

Level 1: Balcony Level 2: Rooftop Level 3: Cliff
🌊

Aquaphobia

Face your fear of water — from a calm pool to the deep open ocean.

Level 1: Pool Level 2: Lake Level 3: Ocean
🌑

Nyctophobia

Conquer the fear of darkness — from a dim room to a dark forest at midnight.

Level 1: Dim Room Level 2: Twilight Path Level 3: Dark Forest

How It Works

1

Choose a Phobia

Select the fear you want to work on from the sessions above.

2

Pick a Level

Start with the easiest level and build up gradually.

3

Choose Your Device

Meta Quest: Click VR icon for full immersion. Laptop: Use mouse + WASD to explore.

4

Breathe & Progress

Use the built-in breathing guide. Move to the next level when ready.
